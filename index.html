About Us

The Plan Studio is a Somerset West based architecture studio founded in 2014. The practice works on a range of residential and commercial projects, focussing on bespoke residential architectural design services.

Our managing partners' combined knowledge of completed projects includes hotels, hundreds of private homes, residential developments, offices, retail and the extension and conversion of historic buildings.

Our design is based on a consistent approach rather than a particular style, and with each scheme a there is a unique response to site, context and our client's brief. We aim to achieve simple, elegant and practical solutions through intensive discussion with our clients and consultant team.
